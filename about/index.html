About Us

Established in September 2023, Cerebra ML provides free, accessible machine learning and AI education for all.

Our Mission

At Cerebra ML, we believe all students deserve access to high-quality education without financial barriers. Our mission is to empower middle and early high school students with a solid foundation in machine learning and artificial intelligence through free, accessible resources and programs. The challenge is clear: premium courses like those on Coursera are expensive, while free options are often too technical for younger learners. As high school juniors who have faced these struggles firsthand, we are committed to creating resources that are accessible, practical, and tailored to the needs of younger students.
